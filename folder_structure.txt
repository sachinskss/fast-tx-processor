main.rs: starts the app, loads config, connects DB, launches server.

config.rs: environment variables and settings.

app.rs: builds the router and shared state.

api.rs: request handlers and response types.

service.rs: business logic, validation, idempotency checks.

models.rs: transaction, account, and request structs.

db.rs: SQLx queries and pool access.

errors.rs: app error mapping.

metrics.rs: simple Prometheus counters and latency histograms.

tests/: integration tests for duplicate requests and balance updates.

Keep the database small
Use just three tables:

accounts: current balance.

transactions: append-only ledger.

idempotency_keys: prevents double-processing.
